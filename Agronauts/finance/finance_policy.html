Finance Policy

1. Purpose

To establish clear guidelines for financial management, reporting, and controls to ensure transparency, accountability, and compliance.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all members of the Finance Team and any employee involved in financial transactions or reporting.

3. Budgeting

  • Annual and quarterly budgets must be prepared and approved by management.
  • All departments must submit budget requests by the end of Q4 for the following year.
  • Variances between actual and budgeted figures must be reviewed monthly.

4. Expense Management

  • All expenses must be supported by valid receipts and approved by a manager.
  • Reimbursements are processed within 15 days of submission.
  • Personal expenses are not reimbursable.

5. Invoicing & Revenue

  • Invoices must be issued within 3 business days of service delivery or product shipment.
  • All received payments must be recorded in the accounting system within 1 business day.
  • Outstanding invoices are reviewed weekly; follow-up is required for overdue accounts.

6. Cash Handling

  • Cash receipts must be deposited within 2 business days.
  • Petty cash funds are reconciled monthly.

7. Financial Reporting

  • Monthly P&L and cash flow reports are prepared and reviewed by the Finance Team.
  • Year-end financial statements are prepared in accordance with applicable accounting standards.

8. Internal Controls

  • Segregation of duties is maintained for authorization, custody, and record-keeping.
  • All financial records are retained for at least 7 years.

9. Compliance

  • The Finance Team ensures compliance with all relevant tax, legal, and regulatory requirements.

10. Policy Review

  • This policy is reviewed annually and updated as needed.
