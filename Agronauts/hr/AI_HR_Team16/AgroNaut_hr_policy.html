AgroNaut HR Policies

  • Purpose: To support employee well-being, work-life balance, and flexibility.
  • Annual Leave: 20 days per calendar year (prorated for new joiners).
  • Sick Leave: 10 days per year. No doctor's note required for up to 2 consecutive days.
  • Public Holidays: As per the official calendar based on your work location.
  • Unpaid Leave: Available upon manager approval.
  • How to Apply: Submit your PTO request via the HR portal at least 5 working days in advance (except in emergencies).
