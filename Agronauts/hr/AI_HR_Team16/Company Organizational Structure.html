I. Leadership Team

CEO/Founder:

  • Responsibilities: Overall vision, strategy, fundraising, major partnerships, and company culture.
  • Reports to: N/A (Founder-led)

II. Core Departments

1. Product Department

Head of Product:

  • Responsibilities: Product strategy, roadmap development, user research, product design oversight, and ensuring product-market fit.
  • Reports to: CEO/Founder

Product Team (Initial hires could include):

  • Product Manager(s): Feature definition, user stories, sprint planning, and cross-functional coordination.
  • UI/UX Designer(s): User experience research, wireframing, prototyping, and visual design.

2. Engineering Department

Head of Engineering:

  • Responsibilities: Technical strategy, architecture, development processes, code quality, and team mentorship.
  • Reports to: CEO/Founder

Engineering Team (Initial hires could include):

  • Software Engineer(s) (Frontend/Backend/Full-stack): Code development, testing, deployment, and maintenance.
  • DevOps Engineer (if applicable): Infrastructure management, CI/CD pipelines, and system reliability.

3. Marketing Department

Head of Marketing:

  • Responsibilities: Brand strategy, lead generation, content marketing, digital campaigns, and public relations.
  • Reports to: CEO/Founder

Marketing Team (Initial hires could include):

  • Content Marketing Specialist: Blog posts, articles, website copy, and social media content.
  • Digital Marketing Specialist: SEO/SEM, social media advertising, and email campaigns.

4. Operations & People Department

Head of Operations & People:

  • Responsibilities: Overseeing daily operations, HR functions (onboarding, policies, culture), administrative tasks, and fostering a positive work environment.
  • Reports to: CEO/Founder

People & Admin Team (Initial hires could include):

  • People Operations Specialist (if needed early on): Supporting HR processes, employee engagement, and administrative duties.
